I am gonna be working on this project/app for 7 days, Its a coding Sprint and it starts today. Its the 1st of april 2026.

My idea is a voice driven daily manager that acts like a helpful friend in your pocket, its an active guide.

You tell it (by voice or typing) what your day looks like (wake-up, gym, work, meeting, study, etc.).
It turns that into a real-time timeline.
All day long it talks to you and says things like:
“It’s 7:45 – time to shower so you leave at 8:30”
“You’re running 10 minutes late – want me to shift your meeting?”
If life happens (you’re late, you finish early), it automatically adjusts the rest of your day.
It feels alive because it uses voice both ways: you speak to it, it speaks back

Core magic: It watches the real clock and guides you moment by moment, not just “here’s your list”.
Who it’s for (your target):
Students, young professionals, or anyone who wants structure but keeps getting derailed by real life.
Why it’s different (your edge):
Normal apps are passive (you have to open them).
Yours is active + voice + smart adjustments → it feels like a personal AI assistant.
